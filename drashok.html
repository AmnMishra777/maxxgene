DR ASHOK MISHRA

  1. Profile

About Me

Dr Ashok Mishra was born on 08/01/1947 at Jigni, Deoria( Uttar Pradesh).He started his career as lecturer Plant pathology at Udaipur(Rajasthan); afterwards he worked as Assoc. Research Scientist and Professor & Head, at Anand Gujarat. He served as Principal and Dean, College of Agriculture, JAU, Junagadh, Gujarat from 2003-2006. He visited Australia twice as visiting scientist during 1991-92 & 1995-96. Presently he is working as Chief Agronomist & Extension Services at Jain Irrigation Systems Ltd. , Vadodara (Gujarat). He has guided 12 PG students. He has promoted UHDP in Mango, Pomegranate, Guava, Dragon fruit and Micro-irrigation in many fruits, vegetables, cereals, pulses and oilseed crops.He has trained more than 1200 B. Tech / M. Tech / M. Sc. Ag candidates for MIS Application and Agronomic practices. He has developed new syllabi and training modules for vocational training of rural youth and other target groups like farmers, bankers, Agric. Officers in Govt., Agronomists etc. Actively involved in training of farmers in micro-irrigation and Hi-tech agriculture, Global GAP and IPM etc. He has played a pivotal role in coordination of PG Research with Universities, ICAR/International Institutes. The responsibilities included collaboration with SAUs, International Research Organizations and training of farmers, scientists and Govt. Officers.He has signed many MOUs viz. 28 SAUs, IARI, CRIDA and ICRISAT.Dr Mishra is highly successful in creating awareness among farmers of Gujarat about advantages of Tissue cultured pomegranate especially threat about bacterial blight. Jain TC pomegranate is tested for freedom from diseases. Yield advantage and fruits cab taken as early as 18 months of planting with good quality & uniformity. Similarly about banana TC farmers he conducted meetings regularly due to which more than 80 lakhs of TC banana is being sold yearly in Gujarat (for the last 3-years approx. 3 crores of TC banana).

He has been awarded with many prestigious awards mainly:

•YL Nene Outstanding Plant Pathology Teacher Award by Indian Society of Mycology & Plant Pathology (2013)

•Pesticides India Commendation Award (2008)

•Crawford Fund for International Agric. Research, Melbourne, Australia (1995 & 1991) etc.

•He was Fellow of Society of Science and Environment.

•He was Associate Editor of Journal of Cell & Tissue Research,( 2005 & 06) and e-Journal of Environmental Sciences (2008 & 09)

•He was member of many important committees like RAC, QRT,

•chairman Board of Studies, member Academic Council, BOM, President (WZ), Indian Phytopathological Society, New Delhi and Secretary, International Working Group of Tropical Virology.

•He has organized 6 training and 4 conferences. He handled 6 external funded projects as PI and two as Co PI.

•To his accomplishments there are many institution building activities like Establishment of wheat pathology laboratory at Wheat Research Station, GAU, Vijapur; Plant Virus Laboratory at B.A. College of Agriculture, GAU, Anand ; Digitized library at JAU, Junagadh, English Teaching Laboratory & established placement cell for students.

•He has acquire life membership of many professional societies.

•He has 150 publications to his account including 52 research papers, 2 books, 9 popular articles , 5 book chapters , 12 leaflets etc.

•He has also prepared Irrigation, Fertigation Schedules for 22 crops and translated in Hindi and Gujarati for crop brochures
